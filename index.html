Your home, on your desk.

A native macOS control panel that consolidates HomeKit, Hue, LIFX, Sonos, TP-Link Kasa, Shelly, Tuya, Ecobee, SmartThings, Govee, Home Assistant, and Alexa-controlled devices into one approachable, comprehensive app.

Stop installing six mobile apps to run your house. The desktop deserves better.

  • Local-first control wherever the protocol allows it.
  • Scenes that run end-to-end on your Mac, not via a cloud chain.
  • "Alexa, run Movie Night" routed through a private Skill straight to your Mac.
  • Privacy-first: cloud tokens stay in your Keychain, no analytics SDKs, no tracking.

Currently in private beta. The Mac App Store listing is in review prep — see the privacy policy for what stays on your Mac and what doesn't.
